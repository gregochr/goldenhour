The story

On the evening of 21st February, I asked AI whether tomorrow's sunrise was worth getting up for. It said yes. I asked what actually makes a great sunrise — the science behind the colour. It told me about cloud layers, aerosol depth, humidity, light direction.

I asked if the weather forecast it was using had any of that data. It didn't. I asked if a tool existed that combined all of it into a simple "go or don't go" answer for photographers. It didn't.

I decided not to risk the early alarm and the wasted drive. Instead, we started building the tool.

PhotoCast uses AI to score every sunrise and sunset across northern England — so you never have to guess whether it's worth getting up.
