Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

PhotoCast is operated by Chris Gregory, an individual based in the United Kingdom. For any privacy-related queries, contact privacy@photocast.online.

What data we collect

When you register for PhotoCast, we collect:

  • Your email address and username (required for account creation)
  • Your chosen password (stored securely using one-way hashing — we cannot read it)

When you use PhotoCast, we may also collect:

  • Your home postcode (if you choose to set a home location for drive time calculations — Pro feature)
  • Forecast outcome ratings you submit (your assessment of whether a forecast was accurate)
  • Usage data such as login times and feature interactions (for service improvement)

Why we collect it

We use your data to:

  • Provide the PhotoCast service (account authentication, personalised forecasts, drive times)
  • Improve forecast accuracy through outcome feedback
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
  • Send marketing emails about new features and photography tips (only if you opt in)

Legal basis

We process your data under:

  • Consent — you provide your information when registering and can withdraw at any time
  • Legitimate interest — to operate and improve the service

Third-party services

PhotoCast uses the following third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

Service What data Purpose
Anthropic (Claude AI) Location names and weather data only — no personal information AI forecast scoring and recommendations

No personal data (names, emails, passwords) is sent to the AI model. Prompts contain only location names and weather data.

When Stripe is integrated for Pro subscriptions, Stripe will process your payment card details directly. PhotoCast will never see or store your full card number.

Cookies and local storage

PhotoCast does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We use browser local storage to maintain your login session (JWT authentication token). This is essential for the service to function and is not used for tracking.

Data retention

  • Active accounts: data retained for the lifetime of your account
  • Dormant accounts: accounts with no login activity for 90 days may be deleted, along with all associated data
  • Marketing email preferences: retained until you change them or your account is deleted

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data — request a copy of what we hold
  • Rectify inaccurate data — update your email, username, or home location
  • Delete your data — request account deletion and removal of all associated data
  • Data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object to processing — opt out of marketing emails at any time
  • Withdraw consent — you can delete your account at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@photocast.online.

Children

PhotoCast is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has registered, please contact us and we will delete the account.

ICO registration

As a UK-based data controller, we are in the process of registering with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered users. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the current version.

Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests: privacy@photocast.online
