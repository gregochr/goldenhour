Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you create a PhotoCast account, we collect:

  • Email address (for login and optional notifications)
  • Password (stored as a bcrypt hash — we never see or store the plain text)
  • Home postcode (if you choose to set one, for personalised drive times)

How we use your data

  • Authentication and account management
  • Delivering forecast notifications (if you opt in)
  • Calculating personalised drive times from your home location
  • Improving the accuracy and relevance of forecasts

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

PhotoCast uses the following external services to operate:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — forecast evaluation and scoring. Atmospheric data (not personal data) is sent for analysis.
  • Open-Meteo — weather forecast data. No personal data is shared.
  • WorldTides — tidal data. No personal data is shared.
  • NOAA SWPC — aurora and space weather data. No personal data is shared.
  • Postcodes.io — postcode geocoding for drive time calculation. Only the postcode is sent.
  • Cloudflare — hosting and CDN. Subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Cookies and tracking

PhotoCast uses a JWT token stored in your browser for authentication. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising cookies, or tracking pixels.

Data retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Forecast data is retained for historical accuracy analysis. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us. If you are in the UK or EU, you have rights under UK GDPR / EU GDPR including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO.

Contact

For privacy enquiries, email: privacy@photocast.online
