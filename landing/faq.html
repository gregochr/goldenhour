Frequently asked questions

What is PhotoCast?

PhotoCast is an AI-powered forecasting tool for landscape, coastal, and wildlife photographers in northern England. It scores every sunrise and sunset across 200+ locations so you know which days are worth the drive — and which are a write-off.

What areas do you cover?

Northumberland, the Lake District, North Yorkshire Coast, Yorkshire Dales, North York Moors, Tyne and Wear, and Teesdale. Over 200 named locations including coastal, mountain, valley, and waterfall spots.

How often do forecasts update?

The planner updates three times a day (early morning, afternoon, and evening). Aurora data polls every 5 minutes during dark hours. Weather warnings update in near real-time.

How far ahead can I plan?

Three days. Weather forecast accuracy drops sharply beyond that, so we don't pretend otherwise.

What's the difference between Lite and Pro?

Lite is free and gives you the planner heatmap, weather triage verdicts, and star ratings. Pro (£4.99/month) adds AI-powered best-bet picks, per-location AI scoring, aurora forecasting and viewline, full score breakdowns, and personalised drive times.

How accurate is it?

Forecasts are based on third-party weather data and AI interpretation. They're indicative, not guaranteed. We find the planner is most reliable for tomorrow and the day after. Three days out is useful for planning but less certain. We never claim to predict the sky perfectly — we just give you much better odds than checking a generic weather app.

Who's behind PhotoCast?

PhotoCast is built and maintained by a single developer with 25 years of experience delivering production systems for global investment banks. It's built to last — not a side project that disappears when life gets busy. The plan is to keep building.

Why do you charge for Pro?

PhotoCast runs on real infrastructure — commercial weather data APIs, AI processing for every forecast, and dedicated hosting. The Pro tier covers those costs and funds continued development. Lite is genuinely free; Pro is for photographers who want the full picture and want the product to stick around.

Is PhotoCast a safety tool?

No. PhotoCast provides forecasts for planning purposes only. Always check official weather warnings and tide tables before visiting coastal or remote locations. Conditions can change rapidly. You are responsible for your own safety at all times.
