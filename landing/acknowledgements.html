PhotoCast is built on the work of many open data providers and open-source projects. We are grateful to all of them.

Data sources

Open-Meteo
Weather forecast data including cloud cover at multiple altitudes, humidity, wind, temperature, precipitation, visibility, and pressure. Air quality data via CAMS (aerosol optical depth, PM2.5, dust).
WorldTides
Tide heights and times for UK coastal stations, enabling spring tide, king tide, and tide-aligned sunrise/sunset detection.
NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center
Kp index, 3-day Kp forecast, OVATION aurora probability model, solar wind data, and G-scale geomagnetic storm alerts.
Light Pollution Map
Bortle class and sky brightness (SQM) data for dark sky assessment at aurora and astrophotography locations.
Postcodes.io
Free UK postcode geocoding for home location and personalised drive time calculation.
European Central Bank (via Frankfurter)
Exchange rate data for cost display.

AI

Claude by Anthropic
AI forecast evaluation, scoring, plain-English explanations, best-bet recommendations, and aurora interpretation.

Mapping

Leaflet
Open-source JavaScript library for interactive maps.
OpenStreetMap
Map tile data. © OpenStreetMap contributors.
